Check certificate on port 443 (HTTPS):

openssl s_client -connect 23.97.151.184:443 -servername 23.97.151.184 </dev/null 2>/dev/null | openssl x509 -noout -dates -issuer -subject

Check certificate on port 25 (SMTP with STARTTLS):

openssl s_client -starttls smtp -connect 23.97.151.184:25 </dev/null 2>/dev/null | openssl x509 -noout -dates -issuer -subject

To verify if the certificate chains to a trusted CA:

openssl s_client -connect 23.97.151.184:443 -servername 23.97.151.184 </dev/null | openssl verify -CAfile /etc/ssl/certs/ca-certificates.crt
